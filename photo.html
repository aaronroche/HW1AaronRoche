Photography

My journey into photography has been truly inspiring. It all started from a school competition where I had to make do with my mom's phone, and now I'm running my own Instagram account. This shows my dedication and passion for this art form.Participating in the Technology Student Association (TSA) was a great learning curve. Placing third out of 50 participants was no small feat, especially considering I achieved this with limited resources. This experience not only gave me exposure but also instilled in me the confidence to pursue photography more seriously. Years later, I've reignited this passion and am sharing my work with the world through my Instagram account, @aaronrochephoto. This step is a testament to my growth as a photographer. Instagram has proven to be a great platform for artists like me to showcase our work, gain recognition, and also learn from the community. As I continue on this path, I remember that every picture I take is an opportunity to tell a story, to capture a moment that speaks to people. I keep exploring, capturing, and sharing. My journey is just beginning and the world is waiting to see through my lens.
